Sync & Share

Mozilla add-on to sync and share your history, bookmarks and tabs.
download .ZIPdownload .TGZ

Welcome to Sync & Share.

This add-on syncs your tabs, bookmarks and history. Want to have the same tabs, bookmarks and history in all your devices? Then download this add-on and try it! Very easy to user and it has a help page if you get lost.

Sign In

There is no need to create an account, as Mozilla Persona is supported by this add-on. You will just have to sign in with your everyday email account.

What was used?

To implement this add-on and the server side:

  1. Javascript.
  2. Mozilla add-on sdk.
  3. Django Framework.
  4. MySQL.
  5. Python.
  6. HTML.
  7. CSS.
  8. JQuery Mobile.
  9. Photoshop: To create the logos.
