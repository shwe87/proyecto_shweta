2.1.Where is the Sync & Share Menu?
Picture 2.1. Sync & Shre Menu.

The Sync & Share Menu will appear when the Firefox browser is open, then touch your device's menu button.

2.2.Menu
Picture 2.2. My Synced Items.

  1. Tabs

    Click Tabs and view all the synced and saved tabs of all your devices

  2. Bookmarks
    Picture 2.3. View your synced bookmarks.

    Click Bookmarks and view all the synced and saved bookmarks of all your devices.

  3. History

    Click History and view all the synced and saved history of all your devices.

  4. Options

    Click Options and view the options available.

    1. Save All Tabs

      This will save all the open tabsin your browser.

    2. Sync All Now!

      This will sync all your devices.

    3. Share

      Opens a tab with sharingoptions.

    4. Help

      Opens this page.
