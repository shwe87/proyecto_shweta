4.Share:

4.1. How to share?

  1. Go to Sync & Share Menu.
  2. Touch Options.
  3. Touch Share Options.

    A new tab will be opened.

Return to menu.

4.1. Sharing Options

First of all, you need to have something synced or something saved manually

  1. Check the items you want to share. Note that you can only check "not folder" items (only urls).

    Here you can delete the items you saved manually. When you delete an item which is being shared then the shared item will also be deleted.

    4.1. Check all the items you want to share.
  2. Choose with whom you want to share:
    1. Share by email address

      You can write your friends' emails separated by commas.

    2. Create a group and share

      You can write your friends' emails separated by commas and then choose a name for the group. If you want to share more items with this group later on, you can do it. See the next step.

      4.2. Create a Group and share with them.
    3. Share with existing group/s

      If you belong to any group, then check all the groups with whom you want to share the items.

      4.3. Check all the items you want to share.
  3. Click Share!

    If everything was OK then you must view a screen like:

    4.4. Everything saved correctly.

    If somethings goes bad, the error message will be displayed at the top of the page.

    4.5. Error message.
    Even when the message is displayed and you don't know what to do then just refresh the page!

Return to menu.
