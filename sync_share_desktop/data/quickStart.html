Sync & Share

Welcome to Sync & Share.

  1. Log in if you haven't already. After you sign in, the syncing starts automatically!
  2. Go to settings.

    Hint: Click Extensions and search for Sync & Share and click preferences.

  3. You can view your synced items from anywhere and any device, just go here. You can view them as well as share them.
  4. If you still have doubts, go to help: Tools > Sync & Share Menu > Help
  5. Now you are ready to Sync & Share!
